About Us

When the Maserati brothers opened in 1914 the Officine Alfieri Maserati in the heart of Bologna, Italy, they could hardly have imagined the impact they would have on the world.
Alfieri, Ettore, Ernesto and Bindo: four brothers united by their unrelenting passion for cars and engines.
Their vision, enthusiasm and expertise turned out to be the cornerstones of what would one day become a global brand, universally recognised as a symbol of motoring excellence.
